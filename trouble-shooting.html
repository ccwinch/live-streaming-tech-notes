Christ Church Winchester: Live streaming tech notes

Troubleshooting Guide

Use the symptom categories below to identify your problem, then follow the link to the fix in Problem Solving.

1. Hardware / Power

Something has no lights / appears completely off

What is off?Go to
ATEM switcherATEM switcher is powered off
Camera controllerThe camera controller is powered off

2. Display / Monitor

A screen is black or shows nothing

What is black?Go to
Multiview monitorThe multiview monitor is black
Streaming PC displayThe streaming PC display is black

3. ATEM Switcher

The ATEM switcher is behaving unexpectedly

SymptomGo to

4. Cameras

A camera is not visible or cannot be controlled

SymptomGo to
One or more cameras missing from the multiview displayThe video of a camera does not appear on the multiview display
Camera controller joystick / buttons have no effectThe camera controller fails to operate the cameras

5. Encoder / Stream

The stream is not reaching YouTube or is not starting

Start here: is the STREAM button on the NVS-34 encoder lit continuously red?

STREAM button state
├── Not lit (white) or flashing red
│   ├── Check encoder input source → see "The encoder stream button flashes red" in problems.md
│   └── Reboot the encoder → see "No stream appears on the YouTube preview" in problems.md
└── Lit continuously red but stream still missing
    └── Check encoder settings → see "YouTube preview says encoder is not configured properly" in problems.md
